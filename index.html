About

The OpenHatch events list is a community that shares notes on about outreach, education, and diversity. We are organizers of free software outreach events, hackathons, project nights, and programming meetups. Most of these are run independently by enthusiastic volunteer organizers; some are run by OpenHatch.

Many of us share ideals of open source, gender and other diversity, and reaching out to newcomers to welcome them to our communities. Some of us run OpenHatch-affiliated events.

We're all here to help and learn from each other. If you're thinking about organizing such an event, have questions about what curriculum to use, or want to share your experiences, join the list and introduce yourself!

(And if you like this list, you might like the OpenHatch blog.)
